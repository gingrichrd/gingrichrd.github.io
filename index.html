PUBLICATIONs

On the Internet No1 Knows Ur a Dog

One of two sister publications investigating the effects of anonymity online. This version is an artifact born of the Internet.

Never Alone

The second sister publication; this version is from an outsider's perspective, born of the analog world.

A two-color risograph printed wayfinding manual, including research and authored poetry. Produced in collaboration with Bex Rohlk.

A spiral bound binary book that uses a spacial and typographic system to explore childhood misconceptions.

An interactive installation in which participants gave structure and meaning to a created glyph-language through their own engagement.  Produced in collaboration with Davis Scherer and Bex Rohlk.

A lightbox poster depicting my ideal final experience. Each bubble is edged with authored stream-of-consciousness writing.
