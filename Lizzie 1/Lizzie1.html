Lizzie Sagt

Lizzie Sagt was primarily a research-based project examining the specific types of language manipulation present in both WWll Nazi propaganda and American ad campaigns aimed at female tweens. The project investigated, both visually and syntactically, where the points of interconnection between the two languages are.

The final outcome was a total of 120 risograph printed 11”x17” posters hung around a floor of a separate, unrelated exhibition.
