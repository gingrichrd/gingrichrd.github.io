Farmfeed

Farmfeed is a proposed app focused on connecting communities to their local farmers and markets. The project consisted of research, screen mock-ups, prototyping, and a final publication.

The final publication is laser printed and Coptic bound by hand as an edition of three.

This project was created in collaboration with Erin Snyder.
